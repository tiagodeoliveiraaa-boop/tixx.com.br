    Property description

    Showcasing Signature Penthouse 601 at One Thousand Ocean, a rare offering that captures the essence of refined oceanfront living. Defined by soaring ceilings of approximately 12 feet, this exceptional oceanfront condominium offers an enhanced sense of volume and light, creating an atmosphere of refined grandeur throughout.This exceptional residence features three beautifully appointed bedrooms, a dedicated office and media room and an oversized laundry suite, thoughtfully designed to accommodate both everyday comfort and sophisticated living. Custom painted and designer wallpapered accent walls add depth and character, complementing the residence's curated aesthetic. The fully reimagined kitchen serves as a centerpiece, outfitted with premium finishes and modern appointments that cater to both culinary enthusiasts and effortless entertaining. Walls of floor-to-ceiling glass frame sweeping views of the Atlantic Ocean, Boca Raton inlet waterway, and city skyline, while inviting natural light to pour into the interiors. Multiple terraces extend the living space outdoors, offering a seamless indoor-outdoor lifestyle and a private vantage point to enjoy sunrise and sunset vistas. One Thousand Ocean delivers an unparalleled standard of service and exclusivity, with 24-hour manned security, concierge services, valet, and on-site management ensuring a seamless and secure living experience. Residents enjoy a boutique, full-service environment designed to rival the finest luxury resorts. Completing this offering, a private oceanside cabana—featuring a lounge area, bathroom, and kitchenette—provides an additional retreat just steps from the sand. Experience the pinnacle of penthouse living where architectural distinction, bespoke design, and oceanfront serenity converge at One Thousand Ocean. The property is being offered fully furnished with artwork included.
