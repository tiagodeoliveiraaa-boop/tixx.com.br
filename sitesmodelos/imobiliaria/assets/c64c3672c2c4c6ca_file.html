    LIVE AT STONE CREEK RANCH

    Indulge in the rarefied lifestyle offered by Stone Creek Ranch, an unparalleled residential community renowned as one of the most coveted and elite destinations in Palm Beach County. With a select offering of just 37 ultra-luxury estates, this prestigious enclave stands as a symbol of opulence and refinement.

    Experience 187 acres of exquisite lakefront living at Stone Creek. This unparalleled location in Palm Beach County, Florida boasts magnificent towering treescapes, glittering waters, and an atmosphere of tranquil serenity, making it a truly extraordinary address.

    As you enter through the private gates of this established gated enclave, you'll discover the ultimate in privacy and luxury living. The impressive stone gatehouse and iconic triple-arched stone bridge provide a breathtaking welcome to this exclusive community.

    A spectacular mile and a half-long promenade winds throughout the property, offering the perfect setting for leisurely strolls, invigorating runs, and scenic bike rides.

    The grounds of Stone Creek have been masterfully designed by renowned landscape architect Krent Wieland, ensuring that each home is embraced by the subtle splendor of nature's endless beauty.

    Whether relaxing in the peaceful surroundings or enjoying the vibrant community amenities, you will find that Stone Creek is a world-class destination that captures the essence of extraordinary lakefront living.

Senada’s utmost discretion and value-added approach to her discerning clientele secured her reputation as the pre-eminent power broker in the United States’ luxury real estate market.     Previously, Senada spent five years as a Top 1 percent producer for Corcoran Group Real Estate. She is a former vice president at Trump International and was the creative force behind marketing Donald J. Trump's condominium developments. Her investment experience includes positions with The Frankel Group, a New York-based strategic consultancy and venture capital firm.

    Senada’s life journey has been extraordinary. Prior to receiving her BA in International Business and MBA in real estate development, she was a survivor of the Siege of Sarajevo and the Bosnian War. In her recent TEDx Talk, "Ingenuity in the Face of Adversity", Senada shared her inspiring story of survival. She is in high demand as a speaker on the subjects of global wealth trends and entrepreneurship.
