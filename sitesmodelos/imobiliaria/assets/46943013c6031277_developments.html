    Development Portfolio

    Senada Adžem collaborates with South Florida’s leading developers, representing a curated portfolio of world-class luxury residences. Her development partnerships include:

    • Empire Development – Renowned for crafting cutting-edge, custom-built estates with a focus on architectural innovation and modern elegance.
    • Alina Residences Boca Raton – A premier residential destination in downtown Boca, blending sophisticated design with resort-style amenities.
