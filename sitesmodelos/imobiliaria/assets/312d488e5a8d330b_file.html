    LIFE AT BOCA BRIDGES

    SPANNING 7 ACRES IN WEST BOCA RATON, BOCA BRIDGES COMPRISES 500 EXQUISITE SINGLE- AND DOUBLE-STORY HOMES AND FEATURES A LAVISH 27,000-SQUARE-FOOT CLUBHOUSE.

    FUTURE RESIDENTS CAN SELECT FROM FIVE DISTINCT HOME COLLECTIONS—WITH SPACIOUS FLOOR PLANS THAT RANGE FROM 2,561 SQUARE FEET TO MORE THAN 8,100 SQUARE FEET OF LIVING SPACE.

    AT THE HEART OF THE COMMUNITY IS THE ALL-ENCOMPASSING CLUBHOUSE, AN EPICENTER OF SOCIAL ACTIVITY, ENTERTAINMENT, DINING AND FITNESS. AMONG THE WORLD-CLASS AMENITIES: A FIVE-STAR RESTAURANT (CUT 5), A RESORT-STYLE POOL WITH A SPLASH PARK AND A POOLSIDE BAR, AN EXERCISE STUDIO—AND A PRO SHOP THAT SERVES BOCA BRIDGES RACQUET CLUB WITH ITS SIX CLAY TENNIS COURTS, ONE ASPHALT COURT AND FOUR PICKLEBALL COURTS (ALL LIGHTED).

    WHETHER YOU'RE DRAWN TO THE TRANQUILITY OF THE COMMUNITY'S SERENE SURROUNDINGS OR THE VIBRANCY OF ITS RICH TAPESTRY OF AMENITIES, BOCA BRIDGES BECKONS AS A WORLD-CLASS DESTINATION THAT REDEFINES LUXURY WATERFRONT LIVING.
