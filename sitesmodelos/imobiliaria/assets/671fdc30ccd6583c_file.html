    Live at the One Thousand Ocean

    The most prestigious address in Boca Raton with superb direct oceanfront views, prime location and luxury lifestyle.

    One Thousand Ocean is a seven story building with a mixture of 49 one and two-story residences with Beach Villas and Penthouse choices, featuring spectacular views of the serene ocean, the vast shoreline, or the vibrant cityscape.

    It is a full service building with hotel like concierge services including manned gated security, onsite management and valet parking. Every home at One Thousand Ocean is lighted by an Ocean and/or Lake Terrace, outfitted with a private plunge pool and outdoor kitchen.

    The One Thousand Ocean building and its one of a kind signature Penthouse was recently featured on Bravo's new hit show, Million Dollar Listing Miami, represented by Senada Adžem.

    One Thousand Ocean is the preeminent leader when it comes to the finest full -service oceanfront living in Boca Raton. The modern building has a curved nautilus shape that is as bold as it is elegant. The sleek wraparound design of the building located on a majestic peninsula gives residences unobstructed panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean, Intracoastal Waterway, Boca Inlet, Downtown Boca Raton and the adjacent Boca Beach Club.

    The residences at One Thousand Ocean range in price from $3 million to $15 million. Floor plans include two to five bedrooms, single and multi-story beach villas, condos and penthouses with living space averaging from 2,800 to over 8,000 square feet with private terraces approximately 1,200 square feet. One Thousand Ocean was recently completed in 2010, featuring only 7 floors and 49 residences designed with light, space and simplicity in mind.

    The ultra modern and chic design of the units, set against the shimmering ocean water and abundant sunlight make for a private peaceful sanctuary. The residences include private elevator entry into the unit, floor to ceiling gallery windows, high ceilings ranging from 10-14 ft in residences and penthouses and up to 20 ft in beach villas. Terraces are customarily accessible from every room and most feature a private heated pool and outdoor kitchen. Residences have gourmet kitchens with custom Italian cabinetry, stone countertops and Dornbracht polished chrome fixtures. State of the art appliances include Sub-Zero refrigerator with wine storage, Wolf natural gas ranges, ovens, microwaves and warming drawers, Julien sinks and Miele dishwashers. Some residences also have butler’s pantries. Master suites have his and hers dressing rooms with vanity dressers, Sub-Zero late night snack refrigerator and freezer and custom millwork. Master bathrooms include large showers and whirlpool spa tubs and appointments of marble and stone. Residences also have marble flooring, fireplaces and multiple balconies. One Thousand Ocean is a full service building with hotel like concierge services including manned gated security, onsite management and valet parking.
