    Prestige Design Homes

    For over 25 years, Prestige Design Homes has been redefining luxury real estate development in South Florida. Led by founder and owner Aldo Stark, the firm is renowned for its commitment to excellence in every facet of homebuilding—from visionary design to meticulous construction and bespoke finishes.

    Prestige Design Homes specializes in creating iconic, custom-built estates that blend architectural innovation with timeless elegance. Their portfolio includes standout properties like Casa Maranello, a Ferrari-inspired mansion in Delray Beach, and Villa Spectre, a Bond-worthy estate, both exemplifying the firm’s dedication to unparalleled craftsmanship and luxury.

    The Prestige team collaborates with distinguished architects and engineers, ensuring each residence is both visually stunning and structurally superior. Every material is sourced to the highest standards, and the team oversees every aspect of the project from inception to completion, delivering homes that rival the finest five-star resorts.

    With a legacy of excellence and a passion for innovation, Prestige Design Homes continues to set new benchmarks in South Florida’s luxury real estate landscape.
