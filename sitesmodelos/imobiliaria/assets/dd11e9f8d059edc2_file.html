    Property description

    A rare offering from the esteemed Mary Widmer Luxury Collection, this modern West Indies estate epitomizes the height of waterfront living in Boca Raton. Set on a lushly landscaped 7,853-square-foot lot, this stunning residence, built in 2018, offers 120 feet of deep-dredged waterfront on an interior canal, just one property removed from the Intracoastal Waterway with direct ocean access. With over 5,600 square feet of air-conditioned living space and more than 6,700 total square feet, this meticulously crafted home blends sleek architectural lines with timeless coastal elegance, creating a sanctuary that is both a visual masterpiece and a haven of luxury.As you step inside, the open flow of space invites you in, where every detail reflects a deep commitment to quality and style. A dramatic metal-and-glass double door entry introduces the home's light-filled interiors, soaring ceilings, and rich materials. Platinum ¾-inch marble floors on the main level seamlessly transition to French white oak upstairs, and a custom-designed staircase offers a striking focal point. The gourmet kitchen, a true culinary centerpiece, is appointed with Subzero and Thermador appliances, including a natural gas cooktop, double ovens, and built-in refrigeration. With an oversized island and custom cabinetry, it becomes the heart of the home, opening gracefully to formal dining and living areas that flow effortlessly for entertaining. The home is fully integrated with Control4 home automation, Lutron lighting, and motorized shades, ensuring comfort and convenience at your fingertips. Upstairs, the primary suite is a true private retreat, offering uninterrupted water views from a generous balcony designed for quiet reflection or relaxed conversation. This serene sanctuary features a wet bar, a spacious walk-in custom closet illuminated by natural light, and a spa-inspired bathroom with double vanities, a dedicated makeup area, and an expansive double shower. It's the perfect place to begin and end each day in comfort and tranquility. Each secondary bedroom is also thoughtfully appointed with custom closets and en-suite baths, ensuring privacy and ease for family and guests alike. The upper-level loft, complete with its own wet bar and Sub-Zero beverage center, provides an inviting space to unwind or entertain with ease. Outdoors, this estate truly comes to life. Imagine dining al fresco on the covered patio while the warm breeze rustles the palms, or taking a refreshing dip in the saltwater, organic shaped pool and spa, finished with Wet Edge Signature Matrix. The pool area is complemented by an EasyTouch 8 control system, a salt chlorine generator for easy upkeep, and a private outdoor shower for added convenience. Whether you're enjoying intimate evenings by the water or hosting larger gatherings, the outdoor spaces offer both privacy and breathtaking views. The dock is a boat owner's dream, featuring a Neptune 20,000 lb. boat lift and a utility pedestal providing shore power and water. With 120 feet of waterfront, there's ample room for a large yacht or multiple smaller crafts, so the water is always just steps away, and every day feels like a getaway. This estate's elevated lifestyle is defined by the details: dual laundry rooms with Electrolux appliances, impact windows throughout, a whole-house vacuum system, and a Generac full-home generator for peace of mind. The 2.5-car garage is meticulously finished with epoxy flooring and includes a dedicated power circuit for electric vehicle charging, ensuring convenience and modern living at every turn. Located in one of Boca Raton's most coveted waterfront communities, this home offers not just an unparalleled living experience but also an enviable lifestyle. Just minutes from world-class shopping, fine dining, pristine beaches, and top-tier golf courses, this residence places you in the heart of South Florida's best offerings. With its blend of elegance, privacy, and spectacular water access, this is an extraordinary opportunity to own a piece of paradise where the best of South Florida living is just outside your door.
